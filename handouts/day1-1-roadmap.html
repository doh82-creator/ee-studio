Course Roadmap

Handout 1 · Day 1 — Orientation  |  Name: _______________________
Over the next 10 sessions, you will move from a broad subject interest to a complete, defensible Extended Essay Research Proposal. Every activity in this course exists to help you produce five things — keep this page and check your progress against it.
Your Destination — 5 Components of the Research Proposal
1
Research Topic 연구주제
The specific, bounded area of inquiry within your subject — not a whole field, but a slice of it.
2
Problem Recognition 문제 인식
The gap, tension, controversy, or unanswered question that makes this topic worth investigating now.
3
Research Purpose 연구목적
What you intend to find out, explain, or demonstrate — the "so that" behind your inquiry.
4
Research Question 연구질문
One focused, analytical, answerable question that will govern the entire essay.
5
Research Methodology 연구방법
How you will gather and analyze evidence — sources, data, tools, and your plan for using them.
