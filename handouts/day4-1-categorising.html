Step 1 · 개별 작성 (10분)
Teenagers today check their phones constantly. They text friends, scroll through feeds, and watch short videos between classes. Parents worry that phones distract students from homework. Teachers report that phones interrupt classroom focus. Some schools have banned phones entirely during school hours to reduce these distractions.
