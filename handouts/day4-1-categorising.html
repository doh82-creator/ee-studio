개념 설명

Categorising(범주화)는 비슷한 생각·대상·개념을 더 넓은 범주로 묶는 과정입니다. apple, banana, cat, dog, grape, elephant라는 6개 단어를 하나하나 기억하는 대신, "과일"과 "동물"이라는 2개 범주로 묶으면 훨씬 쉽게 이해하고 기억할 수 있습니다.

순서범주화의 4단계
1핵심 항목 파악하기 — 반복되는 단어·개념을 찾는다
2공통점 찾기 — 이 항목들이 무엇을 공유하는지 생각한다
3상위 범주 만들기 — 비슷한 항목들을 더 넓은 개념으로 묶는다
4범주에 이름 붙이기 — 그 그룹을 잘 나타내는 이름을 정한다
Step 1 · 개별 작성 (10분)
Technology has significantly changed the way people communicate, travel, and work. In the past, sending letters was the primary means of communication, often taking days or weeks. Today, digital tools like emails, messaging apps, and social media enable instant global interaction, though concerns about privacy, misinformation, and reduced face-to-face communication remain. Similarly, transportation has advanced from horse-drawn carriages to automobiles, electric cars, and self-driving vehicles. High-speed trains and airplanes have made long-distance travel more efficient, but challenges such as pollution, traffic congestion, and infrastructure costs persist. In workplaces, automation and artificial intelligence (AI) have replaced many manual tasks, increasing efficiency in industries like manufacturing and customer service. However, job displacement and the need for new skills create challenges for workers. While technology offers numerous benefits, it also presents ethical and social issues that require careful consideration.
