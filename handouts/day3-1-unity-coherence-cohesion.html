한 단락이 설득력을 가지려면 하나의 생각에 집중하고(Unity), 문장들이 논리적으로 이어지며 (Coherence), 그 연결이 언어적으로도 매끄러워야(Cohesion) 합니다. 오늘 문제 인식을 글로 쓰기 전에, 이 세 가지를 적용해 단락을 고쳐보는 연습을 합니다.
개념 확인
개념설명
Unity단락 안의 모든 문장이 하나의 controlling idea를 뒷받침하는가
Coherence문장들이 논리적 순서(원인→결과, 일반→구체 등)로 배열되어 있는가
Cohesion연결어(however, therefore 등)와 대명사·반복 어휘로 문장 간 연결이 자연스러운가

Social media use among teenagers has increased rapidly. Many students spend hours online every day, checking notifications, scrolling through feeds, and messaging friends before they even get out of bed. Climate change is also a growing concern worldwide, and young people are increasingly vocal about it on the very platforms they use so often. Some studies show that social media affects sleep patterns, with late-night scrolling delaying bedtime and reducing overall sleep quality. Other researchers argue that these platforms actually help teenagers stay connected to friends during stressful periods. Teachers have also noticed changes in classroom attention spans over the past decade. Teenagers should be more careful about their screen time, and schools are now debating whether to introduce formal guidelines.
