Categorising: Topic, Keyword & Paraphrasing

개별 작성 (10분) → 조별 비교 (10분)
아래 글에서 반복되는 키워드를 찾아 상위 범주로 묶고, 핵심 내용을 자신의 말로 paraphrase 해보세요.
Teenagers today check their phones constantly, often reaching for them within minutes of waking up and again right before falling asleep. They text friends, scroll through feeds, and watch short videos between classes, sometimes even during class when teachers are not looking. Parents worry that phones distract students from homework, and many report nightly arguments about screen time limits at home. Teachers report that phones interrupt classroom focus, making it harder to hold students' attention for an entire lesson. Some schools have banned phones entirely during school hours to reduce these distractions, storing them in locked pouches or designated boxes each morning. Other schools have tried softer approaches, such as designated phone breaks between periods, with mixed results so far.
